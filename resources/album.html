Wadi Rum

From February 3rd to February 5th, 2012

Pictures from morning hikes from Wadi Rum. The three first photos are from day 2, where I tried to climb a nearby mountain. It had to turn around half way up, but at least I got a few shots out over the desert. The rest are from the day after where I hiked towards southeast for 3 hours running into local Bedouins before make a loop back to the camp.
